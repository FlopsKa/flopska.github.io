Short 4 Day Trip To Magic Wood

Posted on: So 06 September 2015

Patrick, Lukas and I went to Magic Wood from 24.07 to 27.7 again. Patrick had a car from his parents and we all had some free time and a lot of psyche to try ourselves on some of Avers' classics. With the unspoken goal of finally climbing our first 8a we arrived friday noon and set out to try Foxy Lady (8a) and The Right Hand Of Darkness (8a). Still not able to climb one of those boulders Patrick and I at least wanted to ascent the uber-famous Supernova (7c) while Lukas, having already climbed this gem, wanted to try Jack The Chipper (7c).
